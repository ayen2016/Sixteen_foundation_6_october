Empower through Education

In some cases, it is arguable that education broadens a child’s horizon and benefits the community and subsequently, the country they live in. Many of us will be aware that a child living in poverty or from an impoverished background may have less opportunities and access to educational means. Usually it is the case that extended family members or the generous donor would sponsor the child to qualify for an education of some sort or just accept their fate in life whatever their circumstances.
Did you know approximately 258 million children and youth are without education? How devastating is this statistic. This has been one of our missions to help those who may be struggling. We have started at home, right here in the UK, to assist young people with their educational fees and support families who may be struggling to make ends meet.
Our parents were first generation immigrants to the UK and their sole purpose of mass migration in 60s was purely for their children and their future. We were lucky enough to have an excellent state education, with the 2nd generation fortunate enough to a private one. Education was fundamental in our parents’ beliefs that it would provide a route to success and opportunities in adult life. In most cases they were right, but it was their morals of hard work and determination to achieve good which struck us most.
We hope through our charitable endeavors we are able to support more young people with their educational fees, removing the financial pressure and allowing them to thrive instead.
