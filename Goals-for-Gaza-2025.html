Goals For Gaza Event

Date: 10/09/2024 | Venue: London, UK | Organiser: Araf Gulam

This Summer, we thoroughly enjoyed hosting our first competitive charity tournament as 12 teams went head to head to lift our 1st football tournament trophy for Sixteen Foundation!

Our volunteers ensured smooth operations, referees maintained law and order, and caterers served delicious food. Team Espada won the trophy and Player of the Tournament Award!

Our Highlights

Our volunteers were drilled and ready to take on the event with referees in place for law and order over the games and caterers with tongs at the ready for all things food related. A special shout out to Team Espada for their great form in the group stages and winning the trophy and Player of the Tournament Award.
